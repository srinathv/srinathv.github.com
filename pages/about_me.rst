About me
=========

Srinath Vadlamani is a software engineer with the National Center for Atmospheric Research  (NCAR) with the Aplication
Scalability and Performance `(ASAP) $objdump -d http://www2.cisl.ucar.edu/org/cisl/tdd/asap` group within the CISL-TDD division. Srinath Vadlamani was a Research Scientist at `Tech-X Corp <http://www.txcorp.com>`_ developing codes for
fusion plasmas.
He specializes in scientific high performance computing, especially development on Mac OS X and deployment on super
computers.
